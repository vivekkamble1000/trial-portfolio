  5. Mech Combat Game with AI


    6. I have made the Mech Combat game in Unreal Engine.

    This Game Contains various features such as:-

    1) It has Wave System, Difficulty and no. of AI bots increase as wave no. Increases.

    2) It also has Stamina System, which helps our Mecha to move faster, the stamina decreases after using and refills slowly when not in use.

    3) I have also added Attack System, where our Mecha can use various attack systems when needed, the use of the attack system depends upon the Attack energy, each attack type uses various amounts of attack energy and has Different Damage Points.

    4) In this game the AI Spider bots scan a particular area, if our Mecha comes into the scan area of AI bots, the AI bots start attacking our Mecha.

    5) 2 Types of AI Spider bots are present in our Game.
    I) Bomber AI Spider bot

    II) Turret AI Spider Bot.


    6) I have also added Time fragments which can be collected after killing an AI bot using the traditional method and no special attacks, It is used in our Store system to Upgrade our different Abilities of Mecha and also to refill the Health and Stamina Energy.
